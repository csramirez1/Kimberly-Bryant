Kimberly Bryant

Kimberly Bryant Kimberly Bryant (born January 14, 1967) is an American electrical engineer who worked in the biotechnology field. In 2011, Bryant founded Black Girls Code, a nonprofit organization that focuses on providing technology and computer programming education to African-American girls. After founding Black Girls Code, Bryant was listed as one of the "25 Most Influential African-Americans In Technology" by Business Insider.
